Top_last protocol (run_markov_circuit)

Context
- DW must be False.
- Slab bounds are defined by x0 = max(0, Nx//2 - floor(0.2*Nx)) - 1 and x1 = min(Nx, Nx//2 + floor(0.2*Nx) + 1).
- “Outside slab” means Rx < x0 or Rx >= x1 (i.e., not (x0 <= Rx < x1)).
- “Inside slab” means x0 <= Rx <= x1 for Phase 3/5.
- All sweeps honor the chosen sequence; filtering is applied after ordering.

Cycle counts
- Phase 1 runs for cycles (full sweeps).
- Phases 2–6 run for phase_cycles = cycles // 2 (full sweeps each).

Phases
1) Phase 1 (top mass, OW, full lattice)
   - Projectors: top mass (alpha_1).
   - Sweeps: all sites, cycles times.

2) Phase 2 (trivial mass, OW, outside slab)
   - Projectors: trivial mass (alpha_1 = alpha_2).
   - Sweeps: outside slab only, phase_cycles times.

3) Phase 3 (top mass, OW, inside slab)
   - Projectors: restore top mass (alpha_1).
   - Sweeps: inside slab (including DW columns), phase_cycles times.

4) Phase 4 (trivial mass, OW, outside slab)
   - Projectors: trivial mass (alpha_1 = alpha_2).
   - Sweeps: outside slab only, phase_cycles times.

5) Phase 5 (top mass, OW, inside slab)
   - Projectors: restore top mass (alpha_1).
   - Sweeps: inside slab (including DW columns), phase_cycles times.

6) Phase 6 (trivial mass, OW, outside slab)
   - Projectors: trivial mass (alpha_1 = alpha_2).
   - Sweeps: outside slab only, phase_cycles times.

Cleanup
- Restores projectors to original alpha_1 after Phase 6.
- No local-mode sweeps are used in top_last.
- No mid-column sweeps are performed.
